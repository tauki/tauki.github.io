Wanna know how it feels to fly in the sky? Up high, Blue sky.

Wanna know how it feels to lay down on a cloud? The cloud will stay in position and it does not move around. Or, you like it moving around?

Close your eyes.

I'd risk the fall, Just to know how it feels to fly.
